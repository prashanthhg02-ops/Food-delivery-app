# Food-delivery-app
Food Delivery App: A mobile or web application that allows users to order food from restaurants and have it delivered to their location.
User: A customer who browses menus, places food orders, makes payments, and tracks deliveries.
Restaurant: A business that lists its menu on the app, accepts orders, prepares food, and hands it over to delivery partners.
Delivery Partner: A person responsible for picking up food from the restaurant and delivering it to the customer's address.
Menu: A digital list of food items, prices, descriptions, and images offered by a restaurant.
Cart: A temporary section where users add selected food items before placing an order.
Order: A request placed by the customer to purchase food from a restaurant.
Online Payment: A secure payment method that allows customers to pay using UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, or digital wallets.
Order Tracking: A feature that lets customers monitor the real-time status and location of their food delivery.
Ratings and Reviews: A system that allows customers to share feedback and rate restaurants and delivery partners based on their experience.
Offers and Discounts: Promotional deals, coupons, or cashback provided to help customers save money on orders.
Notification: Alerts that inform users about order confirmations, preparation status, delivery updates, and special offers.
Customer Support: A service that helps users resolve issues related to orders, payments, refunds, or deliveries.
Delivery Address: The location specified by the customer where the ordered food should be delivered.
Admin Panel: A management system used by app administrators to oversee users, restaurants, orders, delivery partners, and reports.
